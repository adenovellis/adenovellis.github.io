What makes a hero?

A hero is someone that inspires others. Heroes dedicate their lives to improving the world through whatever ways they can. Heroes take their passions and turn them into ways to contribute back to society.

A person who I looked up to that used their passion to better the world is Steve Irwin. Steve Irwin's love for animals lead him to be one of the most impactful people for conservation and education about wildlife and the dangers they face. Steve Irwin educated millions of people about the amazing wildlife on this planet and inspired countless people to start their own journey to protect the planet and all of the living creatures on it.
